The saga of Stephanie (daughter of Johann, son of Isak) and Frank to Iceland, homeland of Johann's parents, began July 18, 2005. Here for family and friends, we relate interesting episodes of our trip to a wondrous and faraway land.

The map below shows most of the places we visited. Click on a place name to read about that place and see pictures.
