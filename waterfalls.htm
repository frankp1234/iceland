Iceland has many waterfalls, both large and small. When we were in Reykjavik, Stephanie's cousin Mani and his wife Kristen took us to see the sights outside of town.

One of the places we visited was Gullfoss, a large and beautiful waterfall.

Later in our trip, we came across Godafoss, another big one.
